DIscrete System COmputation

DIScrete COmputation is the analysis of lumped models of physical systems. Modelling requires a good understanding of the physics involved. It reveals a striking equivalence between mechanical, fluidial, electrical en thermal systems. Using FE procedures, a set of (nonlinear) differential equations can be generated and solved rather fast for various initial and boundary conditions.
